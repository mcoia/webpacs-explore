Off Campus Access

Many online resources available through this catalog are free for all users, while licenses for others are purchased for the exclusive use of authorized Missouri Botanical Garden staff and associates. When attempting to access licensed content from outside the Garden's computer network, you will be prompted to login with your Name and your Pin Number. Your pin number will be the same four digit code used when checking out library books from the Garden Library. Your access to all licensed content will persist until you log off or close your browser.

You may also login at any time by clicking on the My Account tab at the top of this screen and entering your name and the same pin number.

If you need help, call the library at 314-577-5155 or email mailto:library@mobot.org.
