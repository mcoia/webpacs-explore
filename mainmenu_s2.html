Innovative Interfaces is pleased to offer this Web-based interface to its catalog of resources.

INNOPAC Web Search Options:

Search the Library Catalog - The Library's Web-based catalog. Search our collection of books, videos, compact discs, and more.

Library Information - Library Hours, Special Programs and Schedules, and other notices from Library staff.

Suggestions and Books to Acquire - Submit general suggestions for the library or collection recommendations to be read by library staff.

Featured Lists - Access library-defined lists highlighting new arrivals, journals, special collections, or featured authors.

View Your Own Record - Find out about items you have checked out, holds status, and fines.

Encyclopaedia Britannica - Connect to and search the Online Encyclopaedia Britannica.
